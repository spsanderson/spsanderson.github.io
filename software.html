healthyR

healthyR: A toolkit for hospital data

Purpose

The beginning of healthyR started from an idea that popped into my head while at work. I was looking for a way to democratize the gathering of and visualization of data in both the administrative and clinical sense. This led me to build out LICHospitalR a package for my employer that is under active development just like healthyR.
